# General Marketing Strategies (Beyond Automation & Conference Plan) - FLAG Internship Exercise (ACTION PLAN - 6)

## Objective
Expand municipal outreach in Texas through authentic local presence, precision targeting, and community engagement — all within a lean $3000 budget.

---

## Extended Marketing Strategy Plan

| Strategy                     | Description                                                                                                                                               | Budget       | Outcome                                                                 |
|-----------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Local Sports Sponsorship | Partner with a Texas municipal or college-level sports event — city baseball, high school football, or community cricket. Sponsor scoreboard or banners.     | ~$1000       | Instantly recognizable brand presence that feels local and supportive. |
| LinkedIn Outreach         | Run hyper-focused LinkedIn ads targeting municipal officers and planners. Include a “Smart City Data Case Study” PDF.                                       | $700–800     | 50–100 qualified leads in 3–4 weeks with high trust-to-contact ratio.  |
| Press & Journal Features  | Publish a success story in Texas Business Journal, Municipal World, or city e-papers. Include QR to Looker Studio dashboard.                                | $500         | 10,000+ targeted impressions and improved conversion credibility.       |
| Referral Incentive Drive  | Launch a referral campaign via n8n or Gmail automation. Offer perks like gift cards, LinkedIn shoutouts, or premium reports.                                | $300–400     | 5–10 organic client leads from trusted municipal networks.             |

---

## Budget
Around ~ $3000


## Why This Matters

Each of these strategies creates **tangible community engagement**, unlike pure digital outreach.  
It blends **local relevance** with **authentic visibility**, positioning FLAG as both **tech-savvy** and **civic-minded**.

---

## Strategic Edge

- Combines **tech-driven precision** (n8n automation, Looker Studio insights) with **human-centric visibility** (sports, media, and community presence).
- Creates **multi-touch recognition** — people see the FLAG name online, in print, and at events, reinforcing trust through repetition.
